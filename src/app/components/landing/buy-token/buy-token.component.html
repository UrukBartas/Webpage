First go to this page and download the Metamask chrome extension, you will need it to connect to major DEX's. Then click "Download now" and the extension would be added to your browser.

Once installed and you click on the foxy icon over the extensions panel of your browser the following window will appear This window will take you through a wizard of setting up your metamask account, it would give you two options "Import seed" or "Create seed", depending on your choice, you may need to store the generated neumonic password safe.

On this step you will have your metamask wallet ready to start messing around on some Ethereum DEX'es, but as Tony Montana (COKE) operates on Binance Smart Chain we would have to configure this custom network. Click over "Ethereum Mainnet" and click "Custom RPC" at the bottom of the networks list.
